I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences, under the mentorship of Bernd Sturmfels. I am interested in algebraic geometry and its applications, in particular to solving PDE and machine learning. My PhD advisor was Anton Leykin.

F3 10, Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences, Inselstraße 22, 04103 Leipzig, Germany
